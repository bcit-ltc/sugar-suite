Reveal

Hidden content can be toggled between a show and hide state by clicking on the button

Active-Reveal

Active-reveal MUST have a data-min-text in order for it to work.

Active-Reveal attribute list:

  • data-button
  • data-min-text
  • data-placeholder
  • data-rows

All of the attributes is optional.

Once revealed, the button won't be disabled even if the input field is empty
