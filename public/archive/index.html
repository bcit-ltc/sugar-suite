Sugar Suite

Sugar Suite is a "Framework Factory" used to produce customized stylesheets designed for building online courses in HTML.

Purpose

Reusability

There is no need to reinvent the wheel every time a new course is developed. By using Sugar Suite, you gain access to a small library of pre-existing CSS configurations.

Consistency

The primary purpose of Sugar-Suite is to ensure consistency across the following dimensions:

  • Readability
  • Behaviour
  • Accessibility
  • Performance

Customization

Although consistency is important, Sugar-Suite is build to accomodate a wide variety of customizations.
