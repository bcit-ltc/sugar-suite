Custom Learning Block

Idea / Innovation

Innovative Business Strategy

Consider implementing a blue ocean strategy: instead of competing in existing markets (red oceans), create uncontested market space (blue oceans) by offering unique value propositions that make competition irrelevant.

Example: Cirque du Soleil combined theater and circus, creating a new entertainment category that commanded premium prices without traditional circus competition.

Standard Learning Blocks

Note

Management Fundamentals

The four functions of management—planning, organizing, leading, and controlling—form the foundation of effective organizational management. Successful managers balance all four functions to achieve organizational goals.

Activity

SWOT Analysis Exercise

Select a company you're familiar with and conduct a comprehensive SWOT analysis. Identify:

  • Strengths: Internal advantages and resources
  • Weaknesses: Internal limitations and areas for improvement
  • Opportunities: External factors that could benefit the company
  • Threats: External challenges and risks

Based on your analysis, recommend three strategic initiatives.

Reflection

Leadership Reflection

Think about a leader you admire in the business world. What leadership style do they exhibit? How do they motivate their team? What can you learn from their approach to apply in your own career?

Key Point

Competitive Advantage

Sustainable competitive advantage comes from resources and capabilities that are valuable, rare, difficult to imitate, and non-substitutable (VRIN framework). Focus on developing unique organizational competencies.

Definition

Organizational Culture

Definition: The shared values, beliefs, norms, and practices that characterize an organization and guide employee behavior. Culture influences everything from decision-making to customer service.

Assignment

Business Plan Project

Develop a comprehensive business plan for a startup venture. Your plan should include:

  • Executive summary
  • Market analysis and competitive landscape
  • Marketing and sales strategy
  • Operations plan
  • Financial projections (3-year forecast)
  • Risk analysis and mitigation strategies

Due: Submit your 15-20 page business plan by the end of the semester.
